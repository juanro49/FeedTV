FeedTV is your personal news and entertainment hub, designed to be fast, lightweight, and private. Read your favorite media via RSS and enjoy a wide selection of open public and community TV channels and radio stations.

Key features:
- Modern and efficient RSS news reader.
- Integrated public and community TV and radio streaming.
- Full support for custom M3U lists: easily add your own streaming sources.
- Powered by open-source projects like RSS-Parser, M3U-Parser, and TDTChannels.

Note: Most preconfigured stations are from Spain. Logos are loaded from external sources.
